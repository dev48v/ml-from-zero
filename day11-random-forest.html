🌲 Random Forest — Many Weak Trees Beat One Strong Tree

Add trees and watch the jagged boundary turn smooth

One deep tree overfits — a blocky boundary chasing every noisy point. Average many trees (each on a random data + feature subset) and their votes smooth into a robust boundary.

Trees in the forest1
Train accuracy—

1 tree = jagged + overfit. ~5 trees = noticeably smoother. 30+ = a clean, stable boundary. Each tree is trained on a bootstrap sample, so they disagree — and averaging their disagreement is the magic.
