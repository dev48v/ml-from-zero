It's a two-line loop: assign points to the nearest centroid, then move each centroid to its mean. ~30 lines.

    
    
      
  1. 1
    Start with k random centroids
    let centroids = sample(points, k);   // k random points to begin
    2. 
      
  2. 2
    Assign: nearest centroid wins
    for (const p of points)
  p.cluster = argmin(centroids, c => dist(p, c));
    3. 
      
  3. 3
    Update: centroid = mean of its points
    for (let i = 0; i < k; i++)
  centroids[i] = mean(points.filter(p => p.cluster === i));
